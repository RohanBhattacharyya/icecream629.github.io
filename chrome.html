Jailbreak

What is jailbreaking a chromebook?

Jailbreaking is when you take out the original software of the computer and add or remove something to make your life more convienent. It is usually done to devices that have limitations to the current hardware or software configurations. In this guide I will be showing you how to jailbreak a chromebook.

Warning!

This may brick your device, so tread carefully. A list of compatibale devices is here. Make sure your chromebook has UEFI checked. If it doesn't, look for a guide on legacy boot. If your chromebook is not listed, you have a much greater risk of bricking your device, but you may or may not be able to continue.
